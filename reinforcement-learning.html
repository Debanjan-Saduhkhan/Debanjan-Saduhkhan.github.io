Research Overview

My research in Reinforcement Learning focuses on developing interpretable and efficient AI solutions for complex business problems. I specialize in causal discovery techniques, multi-agent systems, and policy optimization methods that bridge the gap between theoretical RL and practical applications in user engagement and personalized recommendations.

Key Publications

EFfECT-RL: Enabling Framework for Establishing Causality and Triggering Engagement through RL

ACM CIKM 2024

This paper presents a novel framework that combines causal discovery with reinforcement learning to create interpretable and effective engagement strategies. The framework addresses the challenge of establishing causality in volatile user behavior patterns and uses this understanding to trigger optimal engagement through RL-based interventions.

Key Contributions:

  • Novel causal discovery technique for volatile user gameplay behavior
  • Interpretable deep reinforcement learning-based user-ranking method
  • Framework for establishing causality and triggering engagement through RL
  • Real-world application in gaming industry with measurable business impact

t-RELOAD: A REinforcement Learning-based Recommendation for Outcome-driven Application

ACM AI-ML Systems 2023

This work introduces t-RELOAD, a reinforcement learning-based recommendation system designed for outcome-driven applications. The system optimizes gameplay intensity tailored to user personas, resulting in significant improvements in user engagement and retention metrics.

Key Contributions:

  • Deep reinforcement learning based recommendation system
  • Optimization of gameplay intensity for different user personas
  • 3% improvement in user engagement metrics
  • Personalized treatment generation through RL
