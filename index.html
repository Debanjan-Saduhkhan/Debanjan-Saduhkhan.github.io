About Me

I am Debanjan Sadhukhan, a Senior Research Scientist with a strong background in machine learning, reinforcement learning, and causal inference. Currently, I lead research initiatives at Games24x7, where I work on developing interpretable AI solutions for user ranking, engagement, and personalized recommendations.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science from IIT Guwahati and have postdoctoral research experience from the University of North Dakota, focusing on computer vision and smart energy systems. Over the years, I have contributed to both academia and industry through publications, research proposals, and applied data science solutions. I am passionate about solving complex, real-world problems by bridging research and business impact.
